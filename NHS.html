Diagnostic Analysis using Python:
NHS Healthcare System

Project Context

The NHS is concerned about the utilisation of services, missed appointments, and the potential value of using external data sources such as X. This project aims to evaluate NHS resource capacity and utilisation, identify factors influencing appointment attendance, and assess the added value of incorporating external data sources.

  • Presentation deck

Analytical Approach

Excel and python were the main analysis tools used in this project. Data sets were cleaned,ingested and wrangled using Python.

Three objectives were formed for this project.
1. To evaluate the current capacity status and utilisation of resources in the NHS.
2. To identify the factors affecting appointment status.
3. To evaluate the potential value of external data sources from X.

To achieve Objective 1, several important elements were identified according to the data sets, such as apointment month, service setting, time between booking and etc. To deepen understanding on the subject, additional data sets was acquired from internet, and merged with given data for analysis based on regions and population age. The two online data sets were cleaned using excel, and only necessary information was kept for ease of analysis.

To achieve Objective 2, fishbone was utilised for root cause analysis and illustrated in figure below.

Key Findings

  • More than 60% of capacity of the system had been utilised.
  • Utilisation of capacity exceeded 80% in October, November and March.
  • General Practice had the highest demand across all regions.
  • The top 3 national category were General Consultation Routine, General Consultation Acute and Clinical Triage; two of them are under urgent needs.
  • Monthly appointments are increased with the increase of the population with people older than 50 years old.
  • There were 5% of missed appointments every month.
  • Attendance rate was better when the time between booking and appointment was shorter.
  • Attendance rate was better with appointments done through telephone and video/online.

Recommendations

  • Make the time between booking and appointment shorter because they are more likely to be attended by the patients. Besides, the services needed the most by patients are mostly under urgent needs.
  • Encourage the use of telephone and online/video appointment where appropriate because they have better attendance rate. The use of telephone and online/video appointments should be promoted especially in months where the utilisation of capacity exceeded 80%.
  • Data collection can be done more efficient by standardising the collection methods and categories throughout the England.
  • Further analysis can be done according to regions as the public services, available facilities as well as growth and ageing of population are in different rate.
  • Investigate the effects of pandemic on the capacity of the healthcare system for future planning.
